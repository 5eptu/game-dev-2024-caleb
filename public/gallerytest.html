United Federation Section

United Federation

United Federation is a powerful nation, responsible for defense and attack strategies for the people. They ensure peace across the stars and keep Earth under proper composure.

Fortification Unit

The Fortification Unit specializes in building and maintaining defense structures. They often handle heavy arms weapons being defense structures themselves called "towers" or "fortifiers".

Eradication Unit

The Eradication Unit is made up of CQB specialists and breachers. Their main objective is to pave the way for the army in fireteams.

United Federation Incendiary Division

The Incendiary Unit specializes in wielding flame-based equipment, most notably flamethrowers. They are tasked with burning evidence to eliminate any and all traces of the federations wrong doings.

Marine Corps

The Marines are a rapid-response branch within the United Federation Armed Forces. Equipped with advanced technology and harsher training, they excel in frontline engagements and peacekeeping missions. The marines are the embodiment of the federation's commitments.

Navy

The United Federation Navy is the maritime defense force responsible for coastal security and naval operations. Equipped with latest warships and submarines, it conducts naval warfare, humanitarian missions, and reconnaissance, ensuring the federation's strategic dominance at sea.

Advanced Research & Development

The United Federation's Advanced Research and Development division operates independently of federal control, focusing on a broad range of innovations beyond space operations. They develop advanced technologies in weaponry, cyber warfare, and advanced materials, enhancing military capabilities and ensuring strategic advantages across multiple domains.

Synth Intelligence Agency

The Synth Intelligence Agency is a key branch within the United Federation, specializing in advanced surveillance and intelligence operations. Utilizing synthetic technologies and artificial intelligence, the agency gathers critical information to support national security efforts. Its agents, known as Quiet Suns, employ stealth tactics and high-tech equipment to conduct operations, ensuring the federation stays ahead of potential threats.

Void Special Forces

The Void Special Forces are an elite unit within the United Federation, specializing in covert operations and high-risk missions. Trained in advanced combat techniques and equipped with void technology, they operate in extreme environments, including low-light and zero-gravity conditions. With a focus on stealth, the Void Special Forces are tasked with high-stakes assignments that require exceptional skill.

Task Force COS-22

Task Force COS-22 is a specialized unit comprised of Starborne operatives, dedicated to handling hellspawn reports and anomaly related threats. These elite members have undergone "astral erosion," a process resulting from missteps in the Void, granting them blessed and cursed abilities to confront supernatural entities. Task Force COS-22 are always sent to effectively manage and neutralize hellspawn incidents mostly.

Task Force RIFT-404

Task Force RIFT-404, known as the Four-Tailed Beasts, specializes in the pursuit of individuals and artifacts deemed nearly impossible to capture or neutralize. This elite unit uses void tech to navigate challenging environments and secure high-value targets.

Corporate Section

InkTech

InkTech specializes in developing organic technology, blending biological and mechanical elements. With 89% of their workforce being non-human, the company leverages unique capabilities to innovate and create advanced solutions. InkTech offers protection services to both governments and civilians, positioning itself as a vital player in security and defense across various sectors.

Elgius Inc.

Elgius Inc. once was the largest corporation, fell from grace in its secretive and greedy quest for dominance over Earth. The SIA uncovered their shocking scheme of kidnapping interstellar rockstars, genetically altering them to appear and think like humans. Corporate agents then posed as managers to promote the artists through the Cesco Music Group, highlighting the lengths to which Elgius Inc. would go to achieve their ambitions.

CyberWorks

CyberWorks is a leading technology firm specializing in cybernetic enhancements and advanced artificial intelligence. Known for its innovative approach, the company develops cybernetic implants and systems that enhance human capabilities and improve efficiency in various areas. With a focus on both military and civilian applications, CyberWorks is at the forefront of merging human biology with technology, pushing the boundaries of what is possible.

Xtract Services

Xtract Services specializes in military extractions for both civilian and military clients who are critically injured in various scenarios. With highly trained armed medical professionals, they ensure rapid and safe retrieval from dangerous situations, providing essential medical support during the process. Xtract Services is dedicated to saving lives in high-risk environments, prioritizing efficiency and safety in every operation. First ones in, Last ones out.

Rythen Arms

Rythen Arms is an arms manufacturing company known for producing advanced weaponry and military equipment. Specializing in both conventional and advanced arms, the company focuses on innovation, reliability, and performance. Rythen Arms serves a diverse clientele, including military organizations and private security firms, and is recognized for its commitment to quality and technological advancement in the defense industry.

SWARM Car Company

SWARM is an automotive manufacturer focused on developing autonomous and interconnected vehicles. The company designs cars that communicate with one another to optimize traffic flow and enhance safety. SWARM aims to revolutionize transportation by providing eco-friendly solutions and smart mobility options for urban environments, catering to both individual consumers and fleet operators.

Cesco Music Group

Cesco Music Group is a dynamic music label and production company known for promoting a diverse range of artists across various genres. With a focus on innovation and artist development, Cesco promotes talent and produces high-quality music while leveraging modern marketing strategies to reach global audiences.

Artificial Intelligence Section

CoPilot

CoPilot is a sophisticated AI companion designed for assisting military operations with real-time strategic calculations and support. Mainly used by the United Federation.

Glyph

Glyph is an AI system focusing on visual interpretation, aiding in analyzing enemy tactics by processing complex imagery and video data. Often used by CyberWorks in a lot of cases.

GenoAI

GenoAI is used in genetic research and experimentation, assisting scientists in exploring bioengineering for medical purposes.
