United Federation Section

United Federation

United Federation is a powerful military force, responsible for defense and attack strategies. They ensure peace across the stars, keeping interplanetary threats at bay.

Fortification Unit

The Fortification Unit specializes in building and maintaining defense structures. Their job is to ensure the safety of federation bases and cities.

Eradication Unit

The Eradication Unit is an elite strike force. Their main objective is to neutralize high-priority threats before they escalate.

Corporate Section

Elgius Inc. is renowned for their expertise in mining and resource extraction, contributing to the economic backbone of interstellar trade.

CyberWorks specializes in cybernetic augmentations and bio-tech advancements, enhancing human capabilities in unprecedented ways.
