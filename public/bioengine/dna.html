How Does It Work?

The human body is composed of cells that are programmed to grow into specific organs. By manipulating DNA sequences, we can control how cells differentiate into various tissues and organs. This incubator uses synthetic biology principles to simulate the process.

  1. DNA Commands: Each organ has a specific DNA sequence that triggers cell differentiation.
  2. Growth Speed: You can adjust the growth speed to simulate natural or accelerated development.
  3. Visualize the Growth: Watch the process of cell division and organ formation.
