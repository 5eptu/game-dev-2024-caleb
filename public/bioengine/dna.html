How Does It Work?

This simulator demonstrates the biological process of organ growth from cells. Each organ is developed based on specific DNA sequences which are responsible for the formation of tissues and overall structure.

  1. Select an organ from the dropdown menu.
  2. Click "Grow Organ" to simulate the growth process.
  3. View the generated DNA code for the selected organ.
