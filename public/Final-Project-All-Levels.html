Steps to Import the Twine Archive

  1. Go to the Twine Editor.
  2. In the Twine interface, click on the Library button (found in the main menu). Then, click import. A small box should pop up at the bottom right corner of your screen.
  3. Select Choose File. Select the downloaded Twine archive file from your computer.
  4. Once the file is imported, you will see the full story with all passages in the editor. Double click on Call Of The Federation. Click Build, then Play.
  5. You can click on any passage to view or edit its content. Twine's interface allows you to see the story structure and navigation paths between passages.

Additional Notes

The archive includes all story levels and passages, making it easy to jump to specific points in the narrative or modify sections. You can also see where each passage will take you.
